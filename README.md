### 👋 Hello

Full-stack developer building at the intersection of **Web3**, **AI**, and **security**. Creator of [**web3-agent-kit**](https://github.com/ulsreall/web3-agent-kit) — an open-source Python SDK for autonomous blockchain AI agents, published on PyPI with **986 tests**, **25+ modules**, and **7+ chain** support.

I work across the stack — **Python** for AI/ML backends, **TypeScript/React** for frontends, **Rust/Go** for performance tools, and **Solidity** for smart contracts. Active in **bug bounty** hunting and **DeFi security** research.

*Open to full-time opportunities and collaboration.*
